This is a chess game created by Wei-Han Chou

Main driver is in "Game.java"

Invalid moves will show red square, valid will show green

Invalid:
	Attempting to move a black piece when its white turn (vice versa)
	Take down a black piece with another black piece (vice versa)
	Trying to move a piece to a location it can't either by collision or movement beyond piece scope


Saved files are saved to "MyGame.bin"

Requires the installation of openJDK and JavaFX
